COVID 19 Rates in the US (2020)
Cases per 1000 residents
Map Description: This interactive map shows COVID 19 case rates across U.S. counties in 2020. Hover over a county to see exact rates along with the county name.

Users: researchers, policymakers, and the general public.

Data Sources:
- COVID 19 case rates: The New York Times
- Population Data Used to Calulate COVID 19 rates: 2018 ACS 5 year estimates
- The U.S. county boundary shapefile was downloaded from: the U.S. Census Bureau.
Author: Yosef Birri
Map Projection: Web Mercator (EPSG:3857)
